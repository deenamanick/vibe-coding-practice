### 🔍 Why Testing Matters?

**Testing is your "Safety Net":**
- ✅ **Confidence**: Know that your app works before you show it to users.
- ✅ **Save Time**: Find bugs early before they become expensive problems.
- ✅ **AI Verification**: AI can write code fast, but it can also make subtle mistakes. Testing verifies it's correct.
- ✅ **Prevent "Regressions"**: Ensure that adding a new feature doesn't break an old one.
